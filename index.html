We don't just build projects-we solve problems for good.

Analytics is out of date as soon as it's been delivered. Too often, businesses invest in consulting projects that provide insights but leave them reliant on external expertise to keep those insights flowing. As soon as the consultants leave, their shiny analytics reports are outdated. That's where I do things differently.
I don't just deliver a project - I embed within your team, acting as a principal consultant and trainer, leading a team of your analysts through a structured, hands-on approach.

  1. We identify a high-value project that delivers real business impact.
  2. We select a team of your analysts, eager to build the skills required to deliver on that project.
  3. One day a week, they train and develop the project, following a structured course designed around your business challenge. I provide hands-on guidance and steering, ensuring both project success and skill mastery.
  4. By the end, you have a completed, consulting-standard project and an in-house team capable of maintaining, scaling and repeating the work without external dependency.
This means you get:
  • A tailored project, built to drive your KPIs.
  • Training in the latest data tools and techniques, driven by practical, valuable examples.
  • Consulting-standard outcomes, without the long-term consulting dependence.
  • A cost-effective model, fully offset against your training budget.
  • A repeatable, scalable solution multiplying its value over time.

This is what high-ROI consulting looks like.
